Chicken Katsu Recipe

Description:

Chicken Katsu is one of the most popular side dish from Japan

It is a very versatile side dish which can accompany many dishes such as fried rice

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Corn Flour
  • Black Pepper
  • Breadcurmb
  • Salt
  • Oil
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Take half of a chicken breast and flatten with a mallet
  2. Season with salt and black pepper
  3. Cover chiken with corn flour
  4. Prepare an egg bath by stirring cracked egg in a wide and shallow container
  5. Prepare a coating tray filled with breadcrumbs
  6. Dip chicken in egg bath, then coat it with breadcrumbs (repeat it 2x for a crispy coating)
  7. Heat up a generous amount of oil in a deep pan
  8. Deep fry the chicken in medium heat for about 5-7 minutes
  9. Serve with plain white rice or any other main dish of choice
