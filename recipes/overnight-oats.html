Overnight Oats Recipe

Description:

Overnight Oats is an easy and healthy breakfast menu

It is quick to make and require only a few ingredients

Ingredients:

  • Oats
  • Milk of choice
  • Greek Yogurt
  • Toppings of choice
  • Honey

Steps:

  1. Mix half of a cup each of oats and milk in a container
  2. Mix in about a spoonful each of Greek Yogurt and Honey
  3. Add in your toppings of choice
  4. Close the container and put in the fridge overnight
  5. Enjoy your deelicious breakfast!
