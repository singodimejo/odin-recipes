Indonesian Fried Rice

Indonesian Fried Rice is one of the most popular dish in the country.

It can be found in basically every general local cuisine restaurants.

Ingredients:

  • Rice (a day old is preferred)
  • Shallots
  • Green Onion
  • Garlic
  • Shrimp Paste
  • Dark Soy Sauce
  • Salt (or Soy Sauce)
  • Oil
  • Eggs

Steps:

  1. Chop the Shallots, Green Onion, adn Garlic finely
  2. Heat up pan and put a generous amount of oil in it
  3. Pan fry the aromatics previously chopped and the shrimp paste
  4. Once the aromatics smell fragrant, crack in the egg, mix the egg for a second, and put the rice in
  5. Mix well before adding in the dark soy sauce and salt, then mix well again
  6. Serve it nice and hot! (add an extra sunny side up eggs on the side for perfection.)
